About Me

Hello! I'm Qi Chen, currently pursuing my MSc in Medical Robotics and Image Guided Intervention at Imperial College London. I graduated with a first-class degree in Automatic Control and System Engineering from the University of Sheffield. Throughout my academic journey, I have been passionate about combining system engineering with healthcare technologies and robotics to develop innovative solutions.

My interests range from embedded systems to medical robotics, and I am constantly exploring the intersection of technology and healthcare. My recent projects include wearable healthcare devices and advanced RFID-based systems for smart environments.

Outside of academics, I have a deep interest in astrology, and I enjoy exploring the mysteries of the universe through this lens. My parents have been major influences in my life. My father, an energy engineer, instilled in me a critical and innovative approach to problem-solving, while my mother, with her MBA and CPA qualifications, has taught me the importance of discipline and financial acumen in both personal and professional endeavors.
